ABOUT US

Your Trusted Partner in
Engineering & Contracting

Incorporated in 2018, ERECO-TECH AND SONS LIMITED provides specialized services to manufacturing, oil and gas companies, the private sector, and government establishments across a wide range of technical fields, including Mechanical, Electrical, Refrigeration, and Air-Conditioning Engineering and Contracting.

Our personnel are multi-skilled and multidisciplinary, supported by a flexible management structure that enables us to handle projects across various operational areas. Additionally, we have technical affiliates overseas, providing essential backup support whenever required.

This joint venture arrangement ensures that our projects meet international standards, incorporating the latest available technology. We uphold a strong commitment to quality assurance, ensuring timely and satisfactory project completion. Workplace safety is our top priority. By operating a decentralized organizational structure, we effectively leverage individual expertise, ensuring every project is executed professionally, on time, within budget, and in full compliance with specifications
